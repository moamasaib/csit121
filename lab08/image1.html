L4D2 Party in Elevator

What Type of Image?

This image is a JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group). It supports millions of colors and is best used for photographs, but lacks in certain things like potential animation or transparency.

Why This One?

The first image I chose to use for this Lab Assignment was a screenshot from my Steam profile. I love gaming in my freetime, and on this particular day myself and a group of friends decided to play Left 4 Dead 2 with a mod folder I had assembled. Taken on October 30th, the night before Halloween, it shows our player characters waiting in an elevator. One of my favorite recent screenshots for sure, as it shows a fun session between friends.
