Visual and Media Elements

For Lab:04, I was tasked with creating a multipage site that showed common web graphic formats such as GIF, PNG, and JPEG. Each page had a web graphic dedicated to it, and I even used some personal images of my gaming sessions for two of them. The other was a GIF that I had to reference and give credit to online. It was freeing and quite fun to use images that I had captured myself.

UI and UX

Lab:06 was all about me selecting a website and evaluating it based on the standards of User Interface and User Experience. I selected a simple National Park website and looked at things such as its design (and principles associated with it such as C.R.A.P - Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, Proximity) and how effective it was at meeting the desires of any potential visitor. It helped give me a different understanding about the end product of website design, and how the sites need to be designed for functionality.
