What Type of Image?

As opposed to the first image, this one is a PNG (Portable Network Graphic). Like JPG, it supports millions of colors, but unlike the former, it also supports many levels of transparency. It's the best of the worlds of JPG and GIF, and is well supported by browsers.

I was really into Elden Ring around this time, and thought it would be cool to take a screenshot of my character summoning red lightning. Small as it was, I think I made a good choice that day.
