Remembering everything for structuring a webpage can be difficult. Even though I am fairly certain that I misplaced certain tags and misused others, I feel as though my experience and understanding is still growing. It is not as confusing as it looks!

Some of What I Learned

  1. HTML is used to provide hierarchy and provide meaning for the content.
  2. Structuring content is a fairly elegant process, and can be very personalized. Even though HTML has standards, it is up to the web designer to make use of things like tags to make the website appealing.
  3. Placing elements in other elements, called nesting, can be used to emphasize certain statements.
